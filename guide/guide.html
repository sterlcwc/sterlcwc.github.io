Sites that Publicize your Personal Data

(Generally only applicable to US-residing individuals)

I have always had a knack for online personal investigations. Over my lifetime, many people in my life have asked me to uncover records of their past lives by doing deep online searches. During this work, I've stumbled across many sites that publicize our personal information without our knowledge. My goal is to spread knowledge of these sites and empower people to monitor the data posted about them online. Below are some of the sites I feel everyone should know about.

**Disclaimer: this site represents findings from work funded by a $400 Grant from Cornell's Contribution Project

  • MyLife.com
    This site gathers biographical details on people (birthdates, death dates, addresses, relatives, criminal charges, etc.) and freely displays them online.
    (To get past the sign up wall and lengthy loading process, simply type https://www.mylife.com/firstname-lastname to search for someone's record).
    Information persists on this site long after someone dies.
    See the screenshot I've attached on this page for the record for my deceased grandfather.
